Got Questions?

Who is eligible for your service?

We are currently testing our service and limiting it to customers in the Toronto area.

What are the terms of the loan?

Our loans are backed by the Royal Bank of Canada. They vary in length from 6 to 24 months. The interest rates depend on your financials and the size of the loan.
You will know the exact amount you’ll end up paying before finalizing the loan, so you can make your decision with all the information. 

What do you check in order to decide if I qualify?

We verify your income and bank details. Occasionally we might need you to get a guarantor on the loan.

What happens if my flight gets delayed or canceled?

Once the ticket is booked, it’s like any other ticket you have bought yourself. You as the passenger can deal with the airline directly. However, if the airline ends up refunding the ticket, we’ll refund you your payments. 

When do I start paying?

You pay the loan back in monthly installments, starting when the flight gets booked.
